About

The crew behind the name

Icebrothes is an Icelandic GTA crew forged through friendship, identity, custom builds, events, and the kind of inside jokes that never die. We are not a random lobby group — we are a crew with a shared style and years of history.

The name started as a typo and stuck harder than any polished version ever could. What was once accidental became legendary, and now Icebrothes is a badge people recognize.

Crew Identity

Uniform look. Recognizable presence.

Outfit

  • Black Los Santos cap with LS logo
  • Black reflective sunglasses
  • Black skull bandana covering the lower face
  • Orange t-shirt
  • Black cargo pants
  • Black tactical boots or sneakers

Crew Car

  • Canis Mesa, Merryweather variant
  • Primary color: Orange
  • Secondary color: Black
  • Lowrider rims with white-wall trim
  • Lifted suspension
  • Black bull bars and front bumper reinforcements
  • Roof-mounted light bar
  • Extended fender flares
  • Large off-road tires

Crew Logo

  • Icelandic flag centered
  • Two black fists above the flag
  • Two black muscle cars below the flag
  • A black skull with horns between the cars
  • Represents Icelandic identity, power, and car culture
  • Main crew color is orange
